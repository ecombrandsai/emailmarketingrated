About Email Marketing Rated

We rate every major email marketing platform so you do not have to. Part of the EmailToolAdviser network.

How the rating system works

Every platform is scored on seven weighted criteria: ease of use (20%), deliverability (20%), price (15%), support (15%), templates (10%), automation (10%), integrations (10%). Scores are rolled up to a five-star aggregate. The methodology is identical for every tool we review.

How we make money

Affiliate commissions. We earn a commission when readers click through and purchase. The math doesn't influence the math — several tools we score poorly pay larger commissions than our top picks.
