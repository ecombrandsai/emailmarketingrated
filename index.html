Our seven-criteria rating system

  • Ease of use (20%) — Can a non-technical owner ship a campaign in under an hour?
  • Deliverability (20%) — Inbox placement across Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Apple Mail, Yahoo Japan.
  • Price (15%) — Entry tier cost and pricing predictability as the list grows.
  • Support (15%) — Channels, response time, and resolution quality.
  • Templates (10%) — Quality, organization, and modern aesthetic.
  • Automation (10%) — Welcome series, cart abandonment, birthday, RSS, behavioral.
  • Integrations (10%) — Native integrations with POS, ecommerce, CRM, and analytics.

How is the rating calculated?

Each criterion is scored 1-10 based on observed performance during our 90-day test. Weights are applied (above), then rolled up to a 5-star aggregate. The methodology is identical across every platform we rate.

Are these ratings biased?

Affiliate commissions don't influence ratings. Several tools we rate poorly pay larger commissions than our top picks. The scoring system runs on the same criteria for every tool — the math is the math.
